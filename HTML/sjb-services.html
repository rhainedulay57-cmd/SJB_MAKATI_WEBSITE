WEDDINGS

Reservations

  • Wedding reservations should be made at the earliest convenience, no later than six (6) months prior to the wedding date.

  • A provisional booking is permitted for a maximum of one week. Following this timeframe, a non-refundable deposit of ten thousand pesos
    (PHP 10,000.00) is required to secure the reservation, or it will be forfeited.

Contribution

Our comprehensive wedding package, priced at Php 29,500.00, encompasses the following essential elements:

  1. Ceremony Essentials

    • Wedding Booklets (exclusively for the couple)

    • Red Carpet (center aisle)

    • Nuptial and Unity Candles

  2. Floral Arrangements

    • Tasteful yet basic floral decorations adorning the center aisle

    • Elegant floral displays surrounding the altar

  3. Church Amenities

    • Fully air-conditioned church premises

    • Professional cantors to enhance the ceremony

Requirements

(To be submitted one month prior to the wedding)

  1. Updated Baptismal and Confirmation Certificates of the Bride and Groom, issued no later than six months before the wedding and stamped by the issuing church with "For Marriage Purposes Only".

  2. To ensure the validity of your Marriage License, it is recommended to submit your application at your Municipality roughly four months before your wedding date.
    Please be aware that the Marriage License is only valid for a period of 120 days.

  3. Two 2x2 individual photos of the bride and groom are required.

  4. Banns and permits from the respective parishes of the bride and groom, duly signed by their parish priests, are required.
    The banns should be posted in the bride's and groom's parish churches for three (3) consecutive Sundays.

  5. Photocopies of the CENOMAR and birth certificates of the bride and groom are required.

  6. Complete payment of the wedding contribution is required.

Failure to complete these requirements will result in the cancellation or postponement of the wedding.

Appointments

PUNCTIONALITY IS OF THE UTMOST IMPORTANCE to avoid appointment cancellations. The dates and times of the following appointments must be secured in advance:

  • Canonical Interview

  • Pre-Cana Seminar

  • Marriage Counseling/Confession/Rehearsal
