We are a Salesian Parish

Living the spirit and mission of St. John Bosco — a community that places
the young at the center of its pastoral care.

The modernistic church of St. John Bosco Parish is now one of the most famous landmarks of Makati Central Business District. Its distinctive concave facade features a cross, seemingly riding the crest of a magnificent wave. In the hallowed expanse beneath the wave, thousands of weddings, baptisms and other sacred events have been celebrated for the past years, with the iconic starburst tabernacle upon golden anahaw leaf as dramatic backdrop. Widening ripples above the altar evoke the graces streaming from the Blessed Sacrament and flowing into the vaulted ceiling – a stylized net for the “fishers of men”, arched over the vibrant parish community at worship, a most bountiful catch indeed.

Sa Don Bosco Masaya, Sama-Sama!

Mission & Vision

United in love, service and collaboration, we the Parish of St. John Bosco, Makati, form a Christ-centered and missionary community, with preferential ministry for the youth, the family and the marginalized, Inspired by St. John Bosco and guided by Mary, Help of Christians, we undertake the path of New Evangelization through lifelong faith formation, meaningful liturgical celebrations, uplifting social services and joyful community building.All for the greater glory of the Triune God.
